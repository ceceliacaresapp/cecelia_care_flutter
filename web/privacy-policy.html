Cecelia Care

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026

Cecelia Care ("we", "us", or "the App") is a caregiving coordination application designed to help family caregivers and care teams manage and document the care of their loved ones. We take the privacy and security of your data seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address (provided during registration via Firebase Authentication)
  • Profile photo (optional, uploaded by you)

Care Recipient Data

To provide caregiving coordination features, the App collects health-related information you choose to enter about your care recipient, including but not limited to:

  • Vital signs (blood pressure, temperature, weight, blood glucose)
  • Medication names, dosages, schedules, and adherence records
  • Daily care logs (meals, hydration, mood, sleep, mobility, toileting, skin integrity)
  • Medical appointments and physician notes
  • Photos related to care documentation (e.g., wound tracking, skin integrity)
  • Emergency contacts and medical information

Device Information

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens for push notifications
  • Device type and operating system (collected automatically by Firebase)

2. How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used exclusively to provide App functionality:

  • Displaying care logs, dashboards, and reports to your care team
  • Sending push notifications for medication reminders, shift changes, and care updates
  • Generating exportable care reports (CSV and PDF)
  • Providing AI-powered care suggestions via the in-app chatbot

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or care recipient data with any third parties. We do not display advertisements.

3. AI Chatbot (Gemini API)

The App includes an AI-powered chatbot feature that uses Google's Gemini API through the Firebase Genkit extension. When you interact with the chatbot:

  • Your messages and relevant care context are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate responses
  • Conversations are stored in your Firestore database and are accessible only to authenticated members of your care team
  • Google's Gemini API processes data in accordance with Google's API Terms of Service. Per Google's data usage policy for the Gemini API, prompts and responses sent via the API are not used to train Google's models
  • The chatbot does not provide medical diagnoses or replace professional medical advice

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud provider: Google Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Server location: United States
  • Encryption in transit: All data is transmitted over TLS/HTTPS
  • Encryption at rest: All data stored in Firebase (Firestore, Cloud Storage, Authentication) is encrypted at rest using AES-256
  • Access control: Firestore Security Rules enforce that only authenticated members of a care team can read or write their team's data
  • Photos: Uploaded to Firebase Cloud Storage with access restricted to authenticated care team members via Storage Security Rules

5. Who Can Access Your Data

Your data is accessible only to:

  • You and the authenticated members of your care team (caregivers, family members) that you have explicitly invited
  • App administrators (the primary admin of each care recipient profile) who manage team membership

We do not access, review, or use your care data for any purpose other than providing the App's services and responding to support requests you initiate. No third parties, advertisers, or data brokers have access to your information.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You maintain full control over your data:

  • In-app deletion: Primary administrators can delete all care data for a care recipient from within the App's Settings screen ("Clear All Data")
  • Account deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email address below
  • Data export: You can export your care data as CSV or PDF files at any time from within the App

When data is deleted, it is permanently removed from our Firebase databases. Backups may be retained for up to 30 days before being permanently purged.

7. Children's Privacy

Cecelia Care is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Note: The App may be used by adult caregivers to document care for recipients of any age, including minors. In such cases, the adult caregiver is responsible for the data entered and its appropriate use.

8. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging): Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API (AI chatbot): Gemini API Terms

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
